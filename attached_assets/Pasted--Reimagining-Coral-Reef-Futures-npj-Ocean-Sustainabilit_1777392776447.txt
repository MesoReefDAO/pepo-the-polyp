📄 "Reimagining Coral Reef Futures" — npj Ocean Sustainability (Jan 2026)

This is a structured visioning paper, not a traditional empirical study. A team of coral reef experts co-created three narrative futures to counter the dominant doom-loop in reef science communication. The core argument: bleak outlooks become self-reinforcing and paralyze action — so they built aspirational alternatives instead.

The Three Visions:

🌐 Reeftopia — Earth Stewardship
A post-growth world where governance is restructured around social-ecological wellbeing. Progress is measured by a "Universal Happiness Index" instead of GDP. Coral reefs are planetary commons. Very idealistic, long time horizon.

🌿 Project Reefoir — Biocultural Revitalization
Reefs and coastal cultures rebound after climate-driven die-offs, through investment in refugia, restoration science, and indigenous knowledge systems. Communities share biomaterials and skills to restore both reefs and cultural identity. This one resonates most directly with MesoReefDAO's community-science model.

⚡ The Bay Revolution — Equitable Growth
Community-led, technology-enabled adaptation. Features "AetherReefs" — artificial structures combining renewable energy generation with coral settlement habitat. Emphasizes community tenure rights and sovereign futures. Closest to our modular wetlab + blue economy framework.

Key Concepts That Emerge Across All Three:
• Anticipatory Governance — acting before collapse, not just reacting to it
• Biocultural Revitalization — reef restoration inseparable from cultural identity
• Earth Stewardship — moving beyond extractive paradigms

MesoReefDAO Alignment:
This paper is essentially a philosophical validation of our entire framing. The "Seeds of Good Anthropocene" methodology they use — finding present-day seeds of preferable futures — is exactly what MesoReefDAO's Regen Reef projects represent.
